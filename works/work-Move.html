Move

Move online store - only the best prices. Modern and stylish online store where you can find everything that you need.

ABOUT PROJECT

I wanted to incorporate black, white and red and this was something I decided from the off. This would give it a timeless look and would keep the subtle tone of the brand. Nothing is overpowering but at the same time the red colour is strong enough to give the brand a strong recognisable identity. The second important thing was the logo icon. My choice was a point on the map. Because Move is the place where you will find everything you need. I also created a mobile version of the website which would display on smaller screens.
