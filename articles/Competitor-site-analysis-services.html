Competitor site analysis services

By Amir Aimurzayev on 01 Feb 2020

Smart people learn from the mistakes of others and ... achievements. Also, companies that want to achieve great success can learn from the experience of their competitors. Observing competitors will help not only to identify their strengths and weaknesses, but also to understand what your company should strive for and in which direction to move.

In this article, we will look at tools that can be used to monitor competitor sites. All these tools, depending on the functions performed, are divided into the following groups:

Services for finding brand mentions:

Brand24

The service provides millions of data units from more than 25 social networks, news portals, blogs, forums, etc. Using it, you can find mentions of competitors in these sources and determine the attitude of customers to the company.

Google Alerts

Using Google Alerts, you can track the appearance of brand mentions, the activities of competitors and any other information on blogs and forums in real time. By subscribing to Google Alerts, you will receive email notifications whenever new results for your request appear.

Hootsuite

The service users are more than 10 million people, including well-known companies - NYC and Hershel. The service searches for mentions of competing companies by keywords, provides weekly analytical reports, is great for working with a team (you can create tasks for employees and send personal messages).

IceRocket

IceRocket monitors on social networks (Twitter and Facebook) and on various image sites, but mainly on blogs, which contain about 300 million in the database. The search is carried out in 20 languages, and the results are displayed in graphs.

Backlink Search Services:

Monitor Backlinks

This service automatically searches for competitors' backlinks and searches by keywords, allows you to add filters by the following tags: link status (nofollow, meta nofollow, meta noindex, wrong anchor, not found, etc.), anchor text, page status and domain in the Google Index, sharing on social networks, counting links, etc.

BacklinkWatch

This service is for those who do not have time to register and deal with the interface of analysis tools. Here you just need to enter the site name, captcha and press the button to start the scan. Found pages with backlinks will be gradually loaded into a separate list. It’s better not to analyze large sites with a voluminous link mass - the list will contain a lot of “garbage”, in a heap of which it is difficult to find high-quality sites. But for monitoring small sites, the service is quite suitable.

Majestic Site Explorer

The service performs a mass check of backlinks, compares link profiles and site traffic (you can compare no more than 5 sites at a time), and also searches for keywords or phrases.

Site Positioning Services:

Services from this list determine the position of a site in search engines. Unlike previous services, which are mainly tailored for contextual systems, these track visibility in the "organic".

Alexa

The service allows you to determine the visibility of the site in organic results and get detailed reports on site traffic (sources of traffic to the site, traffic growth rate, number of external links, average time on the site), identify effective keywords, etc. You can use these functions on the site completely free and without registration.

Serpstat

Serpstat is a multi-functional SEO platform for analyzing your competitors, their links and monitoring positions. Explores sites around the world: CIS, America, Europe and Australia by Google and Yandex. Among his clients are: L’Oreal, Yves Rocher, SeoProfy and others.

Social Activity Services:

You can also track how your competitors interact with their target audience through social networks. And also to see what the target audience of your competitors thinks about the company. To do this, use the following tools.

Marketing Grader

Marketing Grader is a free service that gives an overall assessment of the site and provides its detailed analysis, identifying all requests, pages, its download speed and the amount of traffic from mobile devices. This site also evaluates SEO - page titles, meta-description of the site’s pages, etc.

Commun.it

A free service that provides analysis of the social activity of various companies on Twitter. It helps to research information about your customers or competitors, and also provides free analytics on Twitter and keyword searches.

Other services:

There are tools that contain several of the listed functions at once or do not fit into any of the above categories, but will help you analyze the activities of your competitors.

SEM Rush

SEM Rush is one of the most popular internet marketing services. It has already 800 thousand users, including such well-known companies as Philips, PayPal, Forbes, Hyatt. The service searches by keywords, finds backlinks, low-frequency words, searches for competitors in social networks, determines their positions in search engines and searches for ads.

WooRank

The service allows you to conduct a competitive analysis that compares the data of several competitors, checks site optimization and KPI monitoring. It provides detailed site reports containing information about backlinks, keywords, traffic, visitors and their relationship to the site, and weekly notifies users of new data by email.
